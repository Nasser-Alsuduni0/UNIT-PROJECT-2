About ReadWise

ReadWise is a personal project built with passion to help book lovers discover, explore, and enjoy great books.

Whether you're searching for a classic novel, a trending release, or a hidden gem by an underrated author, ReadWise aims to make your reading journey easier and more enjoyable.

The platform uses powerful APIs like Google Books to deliver real-time book information including ratings, covers, descriptions, and more. It also offers helpful filters like category, rating, and pagination to make browsing efficient.

This website was designed and developed by a solo developer — combining web technologies, APIs, and a love for reading — to create a simple, useful, and modern book browsing experience.
